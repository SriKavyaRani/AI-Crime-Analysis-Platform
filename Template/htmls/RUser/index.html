An Overview of Malicious Use and Abuse of AI Technology

AI Crime Typology: Understanding Modern Threats

The capabilities of Artificial Intelligence (AI) evolve rapidly and affect almost all sectors of society. AI has been increasingly integrated into criminal and harmful activities, expanding existing vulnerabilities, and introducing new threats.

This platform reviews the relevant literature, reports, and representative incidents to construct a comprehensive typology of the malicious use and abuse of systems with AI capabilities. Our main objective is to clarify the types of activities and corresponding risks.

We identify four types of malicious abuse of AI: integrity attacks, unintended AI outcomes, algorithmic trading manipulation, and membership inference attacks. Additionally, we explore four types of malicious use of AI: social engineering, misinformation/fake news, advanced hacking techniques, and autonomous weapon systems.
